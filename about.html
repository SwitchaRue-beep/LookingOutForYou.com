Mission

Adolescence is a unique stage in one’s life, the transition from childhood to adulthood. Here teenagers lay the foundation for human development and experience a rapid change and difference in their physical, cognitive, and psychosocial growth. This is the stage where they build lifelong habits that will ultimately affect and set their course of life.

Since teens have immature prefrontal cortex, indulging in bad habits such as a bad diet, inactivity, poor sleep, substance abuse, and excessive screen times is easy but can negatively impact their lives. This is a major issue because laying the foundation is very important for everyone’s life as this is a very sensitive stage and if the future generation is already being built on these reckless behaviour patterns, who knows what our society may look like in the future

This may put the internet in a bad light, assuming that it can not be used for good. We just need to learn how to reach adolescents and appeal to them in a way that promotes healthy living. And what better way to do that than by reaching them through the one thing that they will inevitably use; their devices. Doing this will foster learning and skill building among teens, effectively reaching out to them.
